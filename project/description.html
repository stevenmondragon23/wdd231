IronFocus

Site Name

IronFocus, This name was selected because it communicates strength, discipline, and a clear commitment to fitness goals. “Iron” reflects weight training, while “Focus” emphasizes personalized, goal-driven routines.

Site Purpose

The purpose of IronFocus is to provide personalized gym routines based on the muscle group the user wants to train. The site helps visitors quickly find structured workouts, exercise explanations, and guidance tailored to their fitness goals.

Scenarios

  • How can I find a workout routine based on the muscle group I want to train?
  • What exercises should I do to start improving my fitness and strength?

Color Scheme

  • Primary – #7CFF4D: Used for key highlights such as buttons, active elements, and important call-to-action areas.
  • Secondary – #000000: Used for headings, primary text, and high-contrast interface elements.
  • Accent – #ffecec8e: Used for subtle backgrounds, cards, containers, and light decorative sections.
  • Deep – #00221B: Used for dark backgrounds, footers, navigation bars, and strong contrast sections.

Typography

  • Font Family: Montserrat, sans-serif
  • Global Setting: Applied to all elements (*)
